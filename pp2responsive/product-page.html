Most of our apparel is made from our signature Viscose from Bamboo, and we suggest washing them on cold/gentle cycle inside out. Lay flat to dry for optimal longevity or dry low and cool iron if needed. Follow all care tag instructions.

Viscose is know to be buttery soft and the softer the fabric, the more pilling is common — however, laying flat to dry really keeps the fabric new. Most moms prefer to line dry for optimal texture, lasting color, and to last through the life span of the item.

Due to the delicate nature of our lush fabrics, please do NOT wash with other items with zippers, buttons, or hardware. Snagging may occur. Wash separate with other babies items.

Our signature Päpook® viscose from bamboo is lightweight, breathable, and extra soft for your baby's delicate skin. Its natural weave makes it stretchy enough for a nice snug fit, yet comfortable for all-day wear.

No Harsh Chemicals
We comply with snug fitting standards to eliminate the use of flame retardant chemicals during our production process – irritants like chlorine-containing bleaches, zinc, and sulfate. So you can rest easy knowing our fabric is free of irritants and toxic treatments.

Benefits of Viscose from Bamboo
This fabric has it all. Lightweight and breathable with fibers to wick away moisture while micro-gaps aid in ventilation and heat regulation. This means you stay cooler during the summer and a few degrees warmer during the winter. This makes it great specifically for people with sensitive skin and/or allergies. It also has natural UV protection to protect your peanuts from sun rays in comfort.
